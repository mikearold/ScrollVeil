SCROLLVEIL PRIVACY POLICY
=========================

Last Updated: January 2025


OVERVIEW
--------
ScrollVeil is a browser extension that filters inappropriate images and videos while you browse the web. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data ScrollVeil does and does not collect.


WHAT DATA SCROLLVEIL COLLECTS
-----------------------------
ScrollVeil does NOT collect any personal data. Specifically:

• No browsing history is collected or stored
• No images or videos are sent to external servers
• No personal information is collected
• No analytics or tracking is used
• No data is shared with third parties


HOW SCROLLVEIL WORKS
--------------------
All image and video analysis happens locally on your device. When ScrollVeil analyzes an image:

1. The image is processed entirely within your browser
2. No image data is transmitted to any external server
3. Analysis results are not stored or logged

ScrollVeil uses Chrome's local storage to save your preferences (such as your chosen sensitivity mode). These settings are stored only on your device and are never transmitted elsewhere.


PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED
---------------------
ScrollVeil requires certain browser permissions to function:

• "Access to all websites" - Required to analyze images on any website you visit. ScrollVeil does not track which websites you visit.

• "Storage" - Required to save your filter settings locally on your device.


DATA SECURITY
-------------
Since all processing happens locally on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers, your browsing activity remains completely private.
