How It Works

Protection in milliseconds.

1

Blur first, ask questions later

A CSS shield blurs all images and videos the instant a page loads — before any content renders. You never see anything unfiltered.

2

AI analyzes each image

Our proprietary multi-layer AI pipeline detects people, analyzes poses, classifies clothing, and measures skin exposure. All locally, all private.

3

Safe content is revealed

Flagged content stays blurred until you choose to reveal it. Each image shows a badge with its score — click it for full details about what was detected. Optional auto-reveal is available if you prefer a hands-off experience.

4

You're always in control

Flagged content stays blurred with a "Reveal" option. You choose whether to view it. ScrollVeil never makes that decision for you.

Our Principles

Built differently.

🔐 Privacy by design

Every image is analyzed locally in your browser. Nothing is ever sent to a server. No tracking, no analytics. Your browsing stays yours.

🧹 Ethically trained AI

Our detection models were trained on clean datasets — no one was exposed to harmful content to build this tool. We refuse to use models that compromise ethics for accuracy.

👤 User autonomy

ScrollVeil informs you about content — it never decides for you. Blurred content always has a reveal option. You set your own sensitivity thresholds.

💡 Transparent scoring

Every score comes with detailed reasons — what was detected, how much skin was found, what the language analysis revealed. No black boxes.

Why I Built This

My Story

I grew up during the early days of the internet. Trading magazines with friends at school. Finding the stashes the adults in my life tried to hide. Then came unlimited free internet pornography — and everything changed. It poisoned my entire generation.

After that came social media, and with social media the rise of the influencer. Sex sells, so flash forward to today and practically everything you encounter online is engineered to engage lust. It is relentless, it is everywhere, and for a long time I had no answer to it.

I had to quit social media altogether. And it was only then — through much prayer, through a long and humbling road — that I was finally able to break free from a lifetime of pornography addiction. Praise God, I am finally free.

Looking back, I realized that the internet and social media weren't just passive bystanders in my addiction. They were active contributors. The constant flood of suggestive imagery made real freedom nearly impossible. I had the idea to actively filter this content, but I had no way to make that vision a reality.

Then came AI. And in particular, Claude. With Claude's help I was able to turn that vision into ScrollVeil — an internet rendered as safe from suggestive content as is humanly possible. God used a lifetime of struggle to lead me to this moment.

My goal is simple: help others avoid what I went through, or find the same freedom I found. And to do it for the cost of a cheap coffee. You deserve a clean internet.

✝️
Michael Arold Creator of ScrollVeil
