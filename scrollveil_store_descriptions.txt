DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
---------------------
Browse the web without unwanted surprises.

ScrollVeil is a content filtering extension that automatically detects and blurs inappropriate images and videos as you scroll—on any website, anywhere on the internet.

Whether you're a parent protecting your children, a professional keeping your screen work-appropriate, or simply someone who prefers a cleaner browsing experience free from suggestive content, ScrollVeil works quietly in the background so you can focus on what matters.

Key Features:

• Works everywhere — ScrollVeil filters content across any website you visit
• Automatic protection — Images and videos are blurred in real-time as you scroll
• Customizable settings — Adjust sensitivity levels and choose what gets filtered
• Set it and forget it — Install once and browse with confidence
• Lightweight — Runs smoothly without slowing down your browsing

How It Works:

Simply install ScrollVeil and it starts protecting your browsing immediately. No complicated setup, no accounts to create. If you want more control, open the settings to fine-tune your filtering preferences.

Take back control of your browsing experience. Install ScrollVeil today.
